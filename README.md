# üåç Fix the World with $1 Trillion

An interactive web experience where users spend a trillion dollars on world problems and see the real impact of their choices.

## üéÆ Concept

Can you actually fix the world with a trillion bucks? This gamified experience lets users:
- Choose from 100 real-world spending options (from planting trees to colonizing Mars)
- See the global context and need for each item
- Track their impact as they make purchases
- Learn about tradeoffs between different world problems
- Share their results with friends

## ‚ú® Features

- **100 Curated Items**: From micro-actions ($1K) to society-reshaping megaprojects ($1T)
- **Real Impact Data**: Each item shows global need, impact per purchase, and fun facts
- **Interactive Spending**: Visual budget tracker with animations
- **Category Filters**: Browse by category (Micro, Community, Regional, National, Global Health, Mega, Space, Society, Absurd)
- **Impact Summary**: See your total impact across categories
- **Persistent State**: Your purchases are saved in localStorage
- **Share Results**: Share your spending choices on social media
- **Responsive Design**: Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop

## üí∞ Monetization Ideas

### 1. **Display Ads** (Easiest)
- Google AdSense in sidebar or between categories
- Keep ads minimal to not hurt user experience
- Expected: $1-3 RPM (revenue per 1000 views)
- With viral traffic (1M visits/month): $1,000-3,000/month

### 2. **Sponsored Items** (High Value)
- Partner with nonprofits to sponsor specific items
- Example: Gates Foundation sponsors "Eradicate Malaria" with link to their program
- Charge $500-2,000/month per sponsored item
- 10 sponsors = $5,000-20,000/month

### 3. **Affiliate Links** (Smart Integration)
- Link items to relevant products/donations:
  - "Plant 1,000 trees" ‚Üí Links to OneTreePlanted.org (affiliate)
  - "Buy everyone a Tesla" ‚Üí Links to Tesla with referral code
  - "Fund a pandemic prevention fund" ‚Üí Links to WHO donations
- Charity affiliates often pay 5-10% of donations
- Product affiliates pay 1-10% commission

### 4. **Merchandise** (Brand Building)
- T-shirts: "I saved the world with $999B left"
- Mugs: "I bought everyone on Earth a coffee ‚òï"
- Stickers: Category icons and fun quotes
- Print-on-demand (Printful, Printify) = zero upfront cost
- Margins: $10-15 per item

### 5. **Premium Features** (Subscription)
- **Free tier**: 100 standard items
- **Premium ($2.99/month or $19.99/year)**:
  - Create custom items with custom impact
  - Compare your results with friends
  - See global statistics (most purchased items)
  - Unlock "Expert Mode" with harder scenarios
  - Export detailed impact report
- Conversion rate: 1-3% of active users

### 6. **Educational Licensing** (B2B)
- License to schools for classroom use
- Provide teacher materials and lesson plans
- Charge $200-500 per school per year

### 7. **Corporate Team Building**
